IoT based Video Surveillance Robot

Nov 2019

This is a final project of 8th semester of ECE as per the syllabus of IOE. The goal of this project was to build a affordable and portable video surveillance car robot for remote real time monitoring. This prototype could transmit real time video and display the same in webpage. Also, the robot car could be controlled remotely using the website.

This prototype used Raspberry Pi and Picamera for video streaming and to host our website..

Similarly, this prototype used dataplicity IoT platform to connect Raspberry Pi to the internet.

IoT based Health Monitoring Kit

Nov 2018

This is a mini project of 6th semester of ECE as per the syllabus of IOE. I collaborated with a group of 4 students from same faculty to develop a prototype of a affordable and portable health kit. This prototype could measure the body temperature and pulse-rate of a patient and display the same in webpage. Also, Electrocardiograph(ECG) of patient was displayed using python graph.
This prototype used Arduino as microcontroller and SIM800A GSM/GPRS module was used to send the value of measured body parameters to the webpage.
During the project, I developed the webpage, configured and programmed ECG graph and GSM/GPRS module.
